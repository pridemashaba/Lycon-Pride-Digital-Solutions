About Lycon Pride Digital Solutions

Empowering Businesses Through Digital Innovation

Lycon Pride Digital Solutions is a South African-based digital solutions company focused on helping small businesses, startups, and township entrepreneurs grow through technology. We specialise in building modern websites, mobile applications, and business systems that improve operations, increase visibility, and drive real results.

Who We Are

We are a results-driven digital solutions provider committed to turning business ideas into powerful, scalable systems. Whether you are a small business owner in the kasi or a growing enterprise, we provide the tools you need to compete in today's digital economy.

Our approach is simple: understand your business, identify your challenges, and build solutions that solve real problems.

Our Story

Lycon Pride Digital Solutions was founded with a clear mission — to bridge the gap between technology and small businesses in South Africa. Many township and emerging businesses struggle to access affordable, high-quality digital solutions.

We saw an opportunity to change that.

By combining technical expertise with real-world business understanding, we create systems that are not only functional but impactful — helping businesses streamline operations, attract customers, and grow sustainably.

What We Do

We provide a range of digital services designed to support business growth:

  • Website Development — Professional, responsive websites that showcase your brand and attract customers.
  • Mobile App Development — Custom mobile applications designed to improve customer experience and business efficiency.
  • Business Systems Development — Including ERP solutions, stock management systems, and asset management tools.
  • E-commerce Solutions — Online stores that allow businesses to sell products easily and efficiently.
  • Custom Digital Solutions — Tailored systems built specifically for your business needs.

Founder

Lycon Pride Digital Solutions was founded by Nhletelo Pride Mashaba, an experienced ERP System Analyst and SAP Administrator with over 5 years of experience in business systems, data analysis, and technical support.

With strong expertise in SQL, system optimisation, and process improvement, Nhletelo brings a unique combination of technical skill and business insight — ensuring that every solution delivered is practical, efficient, and results-driven.
